Particle Class

Uses
  • EventHandler
  • Modifier
Defined in: physics/bodies/Particle.js:16

A point body that is controlled by the Physics Engine. A particle has position and velocity states that are updated by the Physics Engine. Ultimately, a particle is a _special type of modifier, and can be added to the Famous render tree like any other modifier.
